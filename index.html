WHY INTELLICURA?

Hospitals are overloaded. Patients wait hours. Intellicura is here to change that. With real time updates, smarter queueing, and seamless flow, we help hospitals run better and patients get care faster.

PHASE ONE

Part A is our fast, launch-ready MVP designed to make hospital visits smoother, faster, and stress-free for patients and staff.

Real-Time Wait Times

Instantly see how long you'll wait at the hospital. Reduces uncertainty and allows patients to plan their visit efficiently.

Digital Check-In

Check in from your phone or tablet. Skip long queues and reserve your spot in the patient queue.

Pre-Registration & Forms

Upload ID, insurance, and medical forms before arrival. Minimizes paperwork stress and speeds up hospital processing.

Patient Notifications

Receive real-time alerts about your slot, delays, or updates. Ensures you never wait unnecessarily and stay informed.

Hospital Dashboard

Staff see upcoming patients, expected arrivals, and hospital load. Enables better patient flow management and reduces bottlenecks.

Analytics & Insights

Tracks average wait times, peak hours, and resource utilization. Helps hospitals optimize operations and improve patient experience.
